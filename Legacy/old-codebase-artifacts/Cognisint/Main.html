AI & Legal Tech for Forward-Thinking Practices

   Cognisint, LLC empowers solo, small, and medium-sized law practices to thrive by automating the parts of legal work that are a grind: processing and triaging incoming mail (both paper and electronic), identifying high-priority issues, conducting routine legal and factual research, drafting and formatting documents for attorney revision, and electronic filing, service, and notification to courts, counsel, and clients.  LexFiat, brings multi-model AI orchestration to streamline workflows with an ethical foundation that prioritizes attorney well-being, client welfare, and justice.
