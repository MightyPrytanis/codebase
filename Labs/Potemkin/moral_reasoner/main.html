Who We Are

Cognisint is dedicated to building human-centered, ethically responsible technology for law and legal automation. We prioritize transparency, fairness, and verified truth, engineering software that amplifies human judgment while honoring dignity and rights.

What We Do

  • Develop AI-assisted workflow and decision tools for legal practitioners
  • Help law firms and organizations implement ethical automation
  • Advise on responsible technology and explainable model design
  • Build open-source projects and collaborate with the legal community

Values

  • Truth & Verification: Prioritizing factual accuracy and always verifying information before acting or automating.
  • Human Judgment: Amplifying—not replacing—professional legal and ethical decision-making.
  • Transparency: Ensuring clarity, auditability, and openness in systems and practices.
  • Fairness & Dignity: Respecting the rights of all, ensuring equity and access.
